Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account or visiting our Track Order page.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, you can return unopened products within 30 days for a full refund. See our Returns Policy for details.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available at checkout. Free standard shipping on orders over $50.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes! All CASAÏA products are 100% cruelty-free and vegan. We never test on animals and our ingredients are ethically sourced from Morocco.
