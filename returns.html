Our Promise to You

We stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return unopened items within 30 days for a full refund.

Return Policy

At CASAÏA, your satisfaction is our priority. We want you to love your purchase. If for any reason you're not completely happy with your order, we accept returns under the following conditions:

Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Products must be unopened and in original packaging
  • Items must be in sellable condition
  • Gift cards and final sale items are not eligible for return

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund to the original payment method. Refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

Please note: Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (damaged or incorrect item).

How to Return

Returning an item is easy. Just follow these simple steps:

  • Step 1: Email us at returns@casaiaskin.com with your order number and reason for return
  • Step 2: We'll send you a prepaid return label (for domestic orders)
  • Step 3: Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  • Step 4: Attach the return label and drop off at any authorized shipping location
  • Step 5: Once received, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement to be sent at no additional cost, and you won't need to return the damaged item.

Please include photos of the damaged item and packaging when you contact us — this helps us improve our shipping process.

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs duties or taxes. We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure your return reaches us safely.

For international returns, please contact us at returns@casaiaskin.com for specific instructions based on your location.

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with any questions about returns or exchanges.
