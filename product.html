Is prickly pear seed oil good for oily or acne-prone skin?

Yes! Prickly pear seed oil has a comedogenic rating of 0 — meaning it will not clog your pores. Its high linoleic acid content (60%) actually helps regulate sebum production and calm breakouts over time. Many customers with oily skin report that their skin becomes more balanced with regular use.

How is this different from argan oil?

While both are luxury Moroccan oils, prickly pear seed oil contains 150% more Vitamin E than argan oil, plus rare betalains that argan oil lacks. This makes it significantly more potent for anti-ageing, dark circle reduction, and skin brightening. It's often called "the new argan oil, but better."

When will I see results?

Most customers notice improved hydration and a natural glow within 3–5 days of first use. Visible reduction in fine lines and dark circles typically appears within 2–4 weeks of consistent daily use. For best results, apply morning and night.

Can I use it under makeup?

Absolutely! CASAÏA absorbs in under 60 seconds, leaving a smooth, luminous base that makes foundation apply beautifully. Allow 2 minutes before applying SPF or makeup. Many makeup artists use it as a priming step for a natural glow.

How long does one bottle last?

With twice-daily use (2-3 drops each time), a 30ml bottle typically lasts 2-3 months. The oil is highly concentrated, so a little goes a long way. Store in a cool, dark place and use within 6 months of opening for optimal freshness.

Is it safe during pregnancy?

Yes, our prickly pear seed oil is 100% natural and safe to use during pregnancy and breastfeeding. It can help with pregnancy-related skin concerns like dryness and hormonal breakouts. However, we always recommend consulting your healthcare provider.
